🧠 What is compound interest?

Interest is extra money the bank pays you just for keeping your money there.

Compound interest is the magic part: next year you earn interest on your money AND on the interest you already earned. Your money makes money, and then that money makes even more money! 🤯

The two secret powers are more years ⏳ and a higher interest rate 📈. Keep clicking Next Year and watch the gold grow — that's how some people become billionaires. 💎
